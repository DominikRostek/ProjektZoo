About event

Christmas is the feast of hearts and lights, and what better occasion to visit Christmas at the Zoo with those you love? For the second time Aalborg Zoo opens its doors for a magnificent Christmas adventure
with animals from all over the world in a star rain of light that guides you through all the world's corners and surprises in the garden.

In a world of Christmas lights

Christmas in the Zoo is fun for all generations with a real Christmas atmosphere in unique surroundings. Join a magical light walk through the beautifully decorated Christmas garden and get a whole new and different experience of the animals and Zoo in the dark. Among other things, you will encounter poetic light landscapes with exotic light animals, a colorful African Christmas atmosphere, an adventurous tropical country and nostalgic Christmas coziness in Denmark's perhaps the only underground Christmas shop. And look forward to a huge surprise when you meet Santa Claus.

Det Gamle Apotek

Beneath the surface of the earth, the Christmas cheer continues. With a look at the curious sea lions and penguins, you can go on an inspiration trip in Det Gamle Apotek, which this year has settled in the adventurous corridors, and will only be found at this address in Aalborg. From here, ice crystals will hang from the ceiling, elves will sit in every nook and cranny, and Christmas decorations will be found in all shapes and sizes, while the animals follow and swim past a treasure chest of a Christmas shop.

The queen of Christmas cake

Can you sense the scent of Christmas cake, it will soon be 5 pm, and
that means that the queen of Danish Christmas cake and patisserie,
Mette Blomsterberg, has come to the cold north, where she will tell
about her cozy, personal Christmas stories and traditions in her
Christmas universe on the Zoofariscenen. Here you will find her every
Thursday until Christmas, where you can also get her new book, Min
Jul, signed with 65 wonderful recipes for Christmas baking.

Then go on a further discovery in Mette Blomsterberg's very own
Christmas cabin, which she has decorated herself. Here you can buy
a selection of her good baked goods and get a recipe for Christmas
goodies with you home as a nice Christmas memory. She will be there
at 26th of November, 3th, 10th,17th of December at 17h and 18.30h.

Buffet

You can of course buy food and delicious Christmas goods at zoo's eateries. In the Christmas Workshop you can enjoy waffles, mulled wine and apple slices. Around the stalls you can be tempted by sweet Christmas items, and at Skovbakken we serve a delicious Christmas buffet with cozy dishes for both children and adults. The Christmas buffet is served twice every evening at 17-18.30 and 18.45-20.15, we recommend that you book a table in advance

Covid-19

We have prepared thoroughly so you can have a good and safe Christmas experience in the Zoo. BUT WE ALSO NEED YOUR HELP when you come to the Zoo. Help us at the entrance by having your ticket ready before you arrive at the checkout. We ask that you keep a good distance from other guests and remember good hand hygiene. If you have symptoms of covid-19, we ask that you stay home. Persons at risk should exercise special caution during the visit.

You must wear a handkerchief in all indoor facilities and eateries, except when sitting down. If you have forgotten a bandage, you can get one for free at the entrance. There will be unification and access restriction in many places in the garden. We will ask you to respect this, as well as other instructions from the staff at Aalborg Zoo. Pay special attention to playgrounds and dining areas so that congestion does not occur.

We regularly follow the authorities 'guidelines and adapt the conditions in the Zoo in line with any changes in the authorities' instructions.
