Christmas Events at Zoo

Det Gamle Apotek

Beneath the surface of the earth, the Christmas
cheer continues. With a look at the curious sea lions and penguins,
you can go on an inspiration trip in Det Gamle Apotek, which this
year has settled in the adventurous corridors, and will only be found
at this address in Aalborg. From here, ice crystals will hang from the
ceiling, elves will sit in every nook and cranny, and Christmas
decorations will be found in all shapes and sizes, while the animals
follow and swim past a treasure chest of a Christmas shop.

In a world of Christmas lights

Christmas in the Zoo is fun for all generations with a real
Christmas atmosphere in unique surroundings. Join a magical
light walk through the beautifully decorated Christmas garden
and get a whole new and different experience of the animals
and the Zoo in the dark. Among other things, you will encounter
poetic light landscapes with exotic light animals, a colorful African
Christmas atmosphere, an adventurous tropical country and nostalgic
Christmas coziness in Denmark's perhaps the only underground
Christmas shop. And look forward to a huge surprise when you
meet Santa Claus.

Covid-19

We have prepared thoroughly so you can have a good and safe Christmas experience in the Zoo. BUT WE ALSO NEED YOUR HELP when you come to the Zoo. Help us at the entrance by having your ticket ready before you arrive at the checkout. We ask that you keep a good distance from other guests and remember good hand hygiene. If you have symptoms of covid-19, we ask that you stay home. Persons at risk should exercise special caution during the visit.

You must wear a handkerchief in all indoor facilities and eateries, except when sitting down. If you have forgotten a bandage, you can get one for free at the entrance. There will be unification and access restriction in many places in the garden. We will ask you to respect this, as well as other instructions from the staff at Aalborg Zoo. Pay special attention to playgrounds and dining areas so that congestion does not occur.

We regularly follow the authorities 'guidelines and adapt the conditions in the Zoo in line with any changes in the authorities' instructions.
